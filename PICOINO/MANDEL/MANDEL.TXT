^YMandelbrot^0

Fractal pattern generator.
Uses both CPU cores.

^WUP^0 .......... move up
^WLEFT^0 ........ move left
^WRIGHT^0 ....... move right
^WDOWN^0 ........ move down
^WQ^0 ........... zoom in
^WA^0 ........... zoom out
^WI^0 ........... integer
^WF^0 ........... float
^WD^0 ........... double
^WP^0 ........... incr. steps
^WO^0 ........... decr. steps
^WBack^0 ........ quit

Blinking LED indicates
render completed.
