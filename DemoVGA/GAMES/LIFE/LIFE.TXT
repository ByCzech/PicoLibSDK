^YLife^0
Cell life simulator
Requires USB keyboard
^WLEFT^0 ..... left
^WRIGHT^0 .... right
^WUP^0 ....... up
^WDOWN^0 ..... down
^WSpace^0 .... flip state
^WEnter^0 .... run life
^WTab^0 ...... next slot
^WEsc^0 ...... quit
Cell rules:
^W3 cells^0 ... born new cell
^W2 cells^0 ... stable state
^Wother^0 ..... dead
