^YMandelbrot^0

Fractal pattern generator.
Uses both CPU cores.
Requires USB keyboard
^WUP^0 .......... move up
^WLEFT^0 ........ move left
^WRIGHT^0 ....... move right
^WDOWN^0 ........ move down
^WPgUp^0 ........ zoom in
^WPgDown^0 ...... zoom out
^WHome^0 ........ incr. steps
^WEnd^0 ......... decr. steps
^WEsc^0 ......... quit

Blinking LED indicates
render completed.
