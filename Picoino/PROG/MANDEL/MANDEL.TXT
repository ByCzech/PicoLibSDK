^YMandelbrot^0

Fractal pattern generator.
Uses both CPU cores.

^WUP^0 .......... move up
^WLEFT^0 ........ move left
^WRIGHT^0 ....... move right
^WDOWN^0 ........ move down
^WSpc+UP^0 ...... zoom in
^WSpc+DOWN^0 .... zoom out
^WSpc+RIGHT^0 ... incr. steps
^WSpc+LEFT^0 .... decr. steps
^WBack^0 ........ quit

Blinking LED indicates
render completed.
